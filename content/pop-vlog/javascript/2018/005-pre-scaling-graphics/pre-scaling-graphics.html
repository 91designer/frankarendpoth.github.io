Pre-Scaling Graphics

This application creates an array of graphics scaled up from their original size every time the screen is resized. This eliminates the need to scale on future redraws. If I redrew the screen at 60fps, I would avoid scaling 60 times per second! That's a huge performance gain! In addition, by storing each tile graphic in its own canvas element, I eliminate texture bleeding upon scaling the image. Texture bleeding occurs when an image is scaled and a pixel needs to be colored based on its neighbors. In a tile sheet, you don't want colors from one tile image bleeding into a neighboring tile image. In addition, this approach eliminates the chance of poor edge reconciliation due to rounding errors and/or antialiasing at tile edges giving the appearance of "gutters" or borders between tiles that should not be there.
